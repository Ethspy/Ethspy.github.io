EPI Design Standards Manual

As the Designer of EPI, one of my major task at EPI was to catalogue and standardize the brand. To further that goal, I created a design standards manual that outlines the guidelines, standards, and best practices for design work within the organization. It covers areas such as visual identity, typography, color, imagery, and layout, and is used to ensure consistency and coherence in all design communications produced by the organization. The manual serves as a reference tool for designers, marketing teams, and other stakeholders, helping them to create materials that accurately represent the organization and its brand.
