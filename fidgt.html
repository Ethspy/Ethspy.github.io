Fidgt Mobile App

Fidgt is a mobile app that I conceptualized and developed the overall visual design. The app is meant to help ease people with fidgeting issues by giving them a non-invasive outlet. The main demographic that had problems with fidgeting were children and individuals on the autism spectrum. To cater to different sensitivities, the app was designed to allow users to tailor their experience with multiple options. The app allowed users to tailor their experiences with multiple options in order to assist users with different levels of sensitivities.

This project was created as an excercise and thus was never launched or programmed but the design prototype can be seen here: Adobe Website.

Client: Design Exercise
Role: Art Direction, Interaction Design, UX/UI
