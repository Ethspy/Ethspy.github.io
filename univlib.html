University Libraries

The University Libraries is the umbrella term for the seven different libraries found on the University of Maryland campus. I worked as the graphics assistant to the graphics coordinator during my college career. The bulk of our work involved updating and creating navigational devices for each library. Other projects involved beautification projects, donor and faculty newsletters, annual reports, video projects, and advertising for library events.

More images and publications can be seen here: Issuu.

Client: UMCP University Libraries
Role: Brand Management, Interaction Design, Print Design, UX/UI
