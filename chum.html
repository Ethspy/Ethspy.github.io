CHUM Website Redesign

CHUM or the Cooperative Housing at the University of Maryland is an all inclusive, non-profit organization that provides affordable communal housing to the College Park area. Due to an increase of traffic from mobile users, the governing committees agreed on a redesign of their former website.

I was asked to recode their website for easier traffic flow and to increase mobile support. The website can be accessed at www.chum.coop and the git repository is located on github.

Client: CHUM
Role: Web design, UI design, UX design
