Get It Done Guide

The Get It Done Guide is the University Libraries' new student booklet that gives an in depth analysis on how the Libraries can help students throughout their college experience. This booklet has been given to every incoming freshman since 2012. To keep the booklet relevant, the design of the booklet is redesigned yearly to include new images and information for each incoming class.

To support the lead designer, I illustrated graphics and editted layouts. The 2016 edition can be found on Issuu.

Client: UMCP University Libraries
Role: Animation, Print Design, Layout Design
