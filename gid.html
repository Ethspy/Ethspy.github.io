Get It Done Guide

The Get It Done Guide is the University Libraries' new student booklet that gives an in depth analysis on how the Libraries can help students throughout their college experience. A copy of the booklet has been given to every incoming freshman since 2012. The design of the booklet yearly to include new images and information for the incoming class.

To support the lead designer, I illustrated graphics and editted layouts. The 2016 edition can be found on Youtube.

Client: UMCP University Libraries
Role: Animation, Print Design, Layout Design
