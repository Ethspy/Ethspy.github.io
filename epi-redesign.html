Epi.org Website Redesign

In late 2019, a decision was made by the Web and Creative team at the Economic Policy Institute to move forward with redesigning and refactoring the EPI website. The back-end of the website had bloated throughout the years and the front-end started to show it’s age. In coordination with the Online and Creative director and the Web Developer, a new wordpress theme was conceptualized.
